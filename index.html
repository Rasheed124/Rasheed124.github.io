Who is Rasheed

a bit about me

I’m Rasheed Tolulope, from a city in Osun state Nigeria, I enrolled at Duromedia Academy 2 years ago as a web developer.

As one skillful among candidates, I have been given the opportunity to be an intern at Duromedia company, a top Digital Marketing Agency in Nigeria located in Lagos.

I’ve developed many websites and apart from that I have been part of a web design project, and also built websites like YDC, Hotlink, etc. After that, I am gonna be developing WordPress themes, plugins, and more javascript projects as soon as possible which I will be updating to my Github account.

It was not an easy task, but I thank God, I gained a lot of experience and a wide range of skills. Am happy to be adept in these languages: HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, with some frameworks like Bootsrap, Tailwind, Jquery, and also the most powerful website design software: WordPress.
